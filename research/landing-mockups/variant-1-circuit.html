The deployment layer

Six primitives. Zero magic.
Everything you can read, reason about, and revert.

otterdeploy is not a black box. Every action is a command you could have run yourself — we just made them composable, observable, and ridiculously fast.

01 / SERVICES

Anything that runs

Dockerfile, Nixpacks, Buildpacks, raw binary. Postgres, Redis, ClickHouse, Meilisearch — provisioned and connected with one command. Persistent volumes that survive your bad days.

02 / NETWORKING

Caddy under the hood

Custom domains, automatic TLS, internal service mesh, and HTTP/3 by default. Wildcard certs in 14 seconds. Private networking that just works between regions.

03 / BUILDS

BuildKit cache that remembers

Layer caching across deploys, branches, and developers. Most teams see 8× faster builds after the first week. Remote builders for ARM, x86, and GPU workloads.

04 / OBSERVABILITY

Logs, metrics, traces

OpenTelemetry pipelines built-in. Grep across services like it's one machine. Flamegraphs for every request. Export anywhere — Honeycomb, Axiom, your S3 bucket.

05 / ENVIRONMENTS

Preview every branch

Pull request opens, environment spawns. Real Postgres, real workers, real domains. Comment posted, slack ping fired, reviewer happy. Auto-destroyed on merge.

06 / RUNTIME

Your hardware, our brain

Bring your own Hetzner box, your own Swarm cluster, your own bare-metal rack. otterdeploy is the control plane — your iron stays yours. Move workloads between providers with a flag.

The dashboard

Every deploy, live.

A board that updates without a refresh. See what's building, what failed, what just rolled back — across every environment, every region. One screen. One source of truth.
