Three pillars

Built for teams who own their stack.

otterdeploy is the layer between your code and your hardware. Bring your own cloud, your own bare metal, or use ours.

Builds that remember.

BuildKit cache stretches across deploys, branches, and developers. The second build is always faster than the first. The fiftieth is faster still.

  • Layer cache hit rate 94% (typical)
  • Cross-arch builders: x86, ARM, GPU
  • Dockerfile, Nixpacks, Buildpacks, raw bin
  • Image streams to registry as it builds

Networking that just works.

Custom domains, automatic TLS, HTTP/3 by default, internal service mesh — all of it managed by Caddy under the hood. Wildcard certs in 14 seconds.

  • Caddy edge with HTTP/3
  • Wireguard private mesh, region to region
  • Internal DNS resolves service names
  • Failover in <800ms across regions

Observability, included.

OpenTelemetry pipelines built in. Logs, metrics, traces from every service, every region. Flamegraphs without an agent. Export anywhere.

  • OTLP-native, no sidecar required
  • Search logs across services in one query
  • Flamegraphs for every request
  • Export to Honeycomb, Axiom, your S3
